Crime, Inflation, & Unemployment Analysis

This is an analysis of crime, inflation, and unemployment datasets. Using Prophet and Simple Model, it attempts to predict valuations into the future.

The combined analysis plots total violent crime, total nonviolent crime, unemployment, and inflation and reviews the data for correlations in the overlapping time periods.
